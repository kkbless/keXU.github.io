Biography

Ke XU currently works with the DMCV Lab of Shanghai Jiao Tong University. He has obtained the dual Ph.D. degrees from Dalian University of Technology and City University of Hong Kong in 2021. His research endeavors aim to design effective visual perceptual models, to align the camera system of AI agents with the capacity of the human visual system in the wild.
